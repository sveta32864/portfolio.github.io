Hello, i am Svitlana

My name is Svitlana Pivovarova. I am 43 years old. I live in Ukraine, in the city of Kyiv. I graduated from the National University of Food Technologies with a degree in "Fermentation and Winemaking", I have a diploma in technology. I worked as a laboratory assistant at Roshen Corporation. From 2015 to the present, I have been working as a quality engineer at the "Plastic Karta" enterprise, which manufactures plastic bank cards for domestic and foreign banks. My responsibilities include checking the quality of semi-finished products and finished products for compliance with the requirements of international standards of the MC and Visa payment systems, for compliance with CQM standards, internal specifications and requirements.
